# POWERFORM - iPhone Installation Guide

## How to Install POWERFORM as an iPhone App

Your workout tracker is now a **Progressive Web App** that can be installed on your iPhone and work just like a native app!

### Installation Steps:

1. **Open in Safari** (Required!)
   - Open `workout-app.html` in Safari on your iPhone
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è This MUST be Safari - Chrome/Firefox won't work for iOS installation

2. **Tap the Share Button**
   - Look for the share icon at the bottom of Safari (square with arrow pointing up)

3. **Select "Add to Home Screen"**
   - Scroll down in the share menu
   - Tap "Add to Home Screen"

4. **Confirm Installation**
   - You'll see the POWERFORM icon and name
   - Tap "Add" in the top right

5. **Done!**
   - The app icon will appear on your home screen
   - Tap it to launch POWERFORM as a full-screen app

### Hosting Options:

To use this on your iPhone, you'll need to host these files somewhere. Here are your options:

**Option 1: Free Hosting (Easiest)**
- Use GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel (all free)
- Upload the 3 files: `workout-app.html`, `manifest.json`, `service-worker.js`
- Access the URL on your iPhone and install

**Option 2: Local Network**
- Run a simple web server on your computer
- Access from iPhone on same WiFi network

**Option 3: Cloud Storage**
- Upload to Dropbox/Google Drive
- Get a public link
- Open on iPhone Safari and install
